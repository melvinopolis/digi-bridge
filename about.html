who we are

As a Teach for America Corps member in 2009, Digi-Bridge founder, David Jessup, Jr., experienced the disconnect between student needs and access to resources in schools. He quickly identified technology as the space where we could move the needle forward in a progressive and impactful way. He created Digi-Bridge out of this realization.

Digi-Bridge is unique in that our solutions are customized and outcome-oriented. We are not associated with a specific manufacturing or software company, allowing us to be hardware and software agnostic.
